Admission Criteria For Class XI

ADMISSION CRITERIA FOR CLASS XI Students appeared in Class Xth (CBSE/ICSE/STATE BOARD/OTHER BOARDS-2020) are eligible for registration.

  • PROVISIONAL REGISTRATION STARTS from 01.06.2020.

  • PROVISIONAL DIRECT ADMISSION in Class XI will be done on the basis of Std IX final term marks/Std X mock test result marks and subsequent online written test and online interview

  • Admission will take on first cum first served basis for limited seats.

Steps for Admission Procedure

Step 1 : Purchase Registration Form. Purchase Registration Form. Fill out the Registration Form and attach all the requisite documents.
Step 2 : Submit the Registration Form -- Online/In person. Admission desk will arrange an appointment with you, for one to one orientation session and a complete school tour.
Step 3 : Admission test will be scheduled (from grade 2 onwards).
Step 4 : Admission Test records will be reviewed by the Principal & a decision regarding the Admission will be taken.
Step 5 : On receiving the letter of invitation to join the school, parents need to pay the fees to block the seat. On receiving the payment, and all required documents admission process will be deemed to be completed.
